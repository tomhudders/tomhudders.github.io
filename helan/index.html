— About the project

In collaboration with the creative team and the front-end developers on both our and the client side we built a design system in Storybook. This contained all the necessary building blocks, patterns and example pages to create product offers, landing pages and a comprehensive knowledge database.

Design system

— Product offers and information

The site has two distinct types of content: Helan's offers and info about a variety of subjects. Whether you are looking for dental insurance or need info about administration concerning sick leave, it should be easy to find. This presented quite a few UX/UI challenges as we were merging 2 companies (Partena and OZ) so a lot of time went into creating the sitemap and menu structure.
