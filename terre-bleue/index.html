Terre Bleue

My role

— About the project

The e-commerce platform is an extention of the physical boutiques that can be found all over Flanders. So the goal of the platform is two-fold: to both inspire potential customers and provide a smooth and easy shopping experience.
There are numerous integrations to follow up on sales and make it easy for Terre Bleue to launch new clothing collections every 6 months.

— Ensuring growth

Over the past years more and more functionality and templates have been added and Terre Bleue now has a library of over 45 design elements to build productpages, landingpages and e-mail templates. All new templates had to be integrated seamlessly in the existing design and codebase.

In 2021 Terre Bleue acquired two new clothing brands; Gigue and Zilton. This year Gigue was launched with the same backend and codebase structure as the Terre Bleue site but with a new branding.
